Welcome to the world of Frankline Alele Ambetsa

A versatile professional with a multifaceted skill set that spans across various domains of engineering, programming, and data science.

With a solid foundation in Civil Engineering, Frankline has journeyed through the realms of Software Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, and beyond. His academic pursuits have been diverse, encompassing a wide array of courses that have enriched his knowledge and expertise.

In the realm of Civil/Structural Engineering, Frankline is a seasoned practitioner, adept in utilizing an extensive toolkit of software and tools including Autodesk AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Fusion, Revit, MatLab, and many more. His proficiency extends to structural analysis and design with software like StaadPro, CSI Etabs, SAP 2000, and Ansys, particularly in the realm of aeronautics. His expertise in Quantity Surveying and Land Surveying further complements his engineering prowess, supported by tools such as BlueBeam, Microsoft Excel, Total Station, and TerraSync.

Frankline's architectural endeavors are marked by proficiency in Graphisoft ArchiCad, Lumion, Atlantis, and SketchUP, enabling him to bring designs to life with creativity and precision. In the domain of programming and software engineering, he's equipped with languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Dart, and Flutter, alongside a strong grasp of databases and languages like Java, C++, C#, and C.

Data holds no secrets for Frankline, as he navigates through the intricacies of data science and analytics using tools such as IBM SPSS, RStudio, SAS, Excel, Python, and Power BI. His expertise extends to robotics and machine learning, where he applies tools like MatLab Robotics ToolBox, Python, Autodesk Inventor, and UiPath to develop innovative solutions.

Frankline's academic journey has also seen him delve into the world of academic writing, crafting research papers, dissertations, and essays with finesse, particularly in STEM subjects.
